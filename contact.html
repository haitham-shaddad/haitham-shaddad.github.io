Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive software development, IT consulting, outsourcing services, and technical support. Our expertise includes custom application development, cloud migration, enterprise solutions, and more.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. Small projects may take 4-8 weeks, while larger enterprise applications can take 3-6 months or more. We'll provide a detailed timeline during the initial consultation.

What is your development process?

We follow an Agile methodology with iterative development cycles. Our process includes discovery, design, development, testing, and deployment phases with regular client feedback and demonstrations.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive support and maintenance services including 24/7 monitoring, bug fixes, security updates, and feature enhancements to keep your applications running smoothly.

What industries do you serve?

We serve clients across multiple industries including healthcare, finance, retail, manufacturing, logistics, and more. Our experience spans over 15 different industry verticals.

Can you work with our existing team?

Absolutely! We offer staff augmentation services where our developers integrate seamlessly with your existing team. We can also provide dedicated teams or handle complete project outsourcing.
