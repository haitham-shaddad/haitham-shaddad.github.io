Port Waratah Coal Services (PWCS) is a key link in the Hunter Valley Coal Chain, handling the export of coal from the Hunter Valley region, one of the world's largest coal-producing areas. PWCS operates Carrington Coal Terminal and Kooragang Coal Terminal, providing comprehensive services including coal receival, blending, stockpiling, and shiploading.

Enterprise Platform

Services Portal

Foundational platform for PWCS coal export operations

Project Overview

The Services Portal serves as the foundational platform for PWCS operations, facilitating the management of customers, contracts, coal brands, and designated loading points for each brand. Services Assurance Officers and the Shipment Contracts team leverage the portal to manage diverse vessels and their respective trips, as well as to orchestrate the coal loading plan for each vessel trip.

Challenge

  • Legacy system developed over 10 years ago requiring modernization
  • Complex vessel trip management and coal loading orchestration
  • Need for cloud migration and single sign-on implementation
  • Continuous system maintenance while delivering enhancements

Solution

  • Ongoing support and maintenance since 2021
  • Successful migration to Microsoft Azure cloud platform
  • Implementation of Single Sign-On (SSO) for enhanced security
  • Strategic enhancements optimizing efficiency for employees and customers
  • Bug resolution and system stability improvements

Mobile Innovation

Mobile JSEA & Take5

AI-powered mobile risk assessment application

Project Overview

AIM developed a mobile-first web application to enable PWCS employees and contractors to create and sign both JSEA (Job Safety and Environment Analysis) and Take5 risk assessments. The app leverages cutting-edge AI technology to provide a seamless user experience with handwriting recognition and speech-to-text functionality.

PWCS utilizes two primary risk assessment methods: Take5 - a simplified, quick assessment suitable for routine tasks with yes/no questions, and JSEA - a detailed, step-by-step analysis breaking down tasks into individual steps with hazard identification and controls.

Challenge

  • Paper-based risk assessment process causing delays and inefficiencies
  • Need for mobile solution accessible in field conditions
  • Support for both quick Take5 and detailed JSEA assessments
  • Digital signature capture for compliance and audit trails

Solution

  • Mobile-first responsive web application
  • AI-powered handwriting-to-text conversion
  • Speech-to-text functionality for hands-free input
  • Digital signature capture and validation
  • Support for both JSEA and Take5 assessment types
